Systems that create control, visibility, and consistent follow-ups. Designed to reduce chaos for teams and improve customer experience.

Lead & inquiry tracking

Capture leads from WhatsApp, calls, and forms. Assign owners, track stages, and ensure follow-ups happen on time.

Customer care ticketing

Dashboards for owners

Real-time visibility on leads, pending requests, response times, and team performance—without micromanagement.
